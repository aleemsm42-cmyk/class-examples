Educational Context

Audience: 6th Grade Math

TEKS Alignment: 6.7(A) & CCSS.MATH.CONTENT.6.EE.A.2.C - Equivalent Expressions & Order of Operations (PEMDAS).

Description: This interactive, 10-level challenge allows students to navigate sequence-based math problems to restore a spaceship's systems. It includes a cascading "lives" mechanic where incorrect operations drain system integrity, eventually forcing a reboot. It serves as an engaging, high-stakes "Check for Understanding" during an expressions and equations unit, culminating in an Algebra 1 "Boss Level."
