Educational Context

Audience: 6th Grade Science

TEKS Alignment: 6.12(E) & 6.12(B) - Biotic and Abiotic Interactions.

Description: This branching exercise allows students to manipulate abiotic factors (water/soil) to see the immediate and long-term effects on biotic populations (lions/prey). It includes cascading ecological logic (e.g., if water drops too low, prey populations automatically crash) and serves as an excellent "Check for Understanding" during an ecosystem stability unit.
